Lasagna

A plate of home-made lasagna

Lasagna can be made a variety of ways, but this recipe resembles the style many of us Americans know and love.

Meaty, cheesy, saucy, noodle-y goodness awaits us at the end of this journey.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage and ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Parsley, dried basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Home made lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta
  • An egg

Steps

  1. make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
